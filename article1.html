Published on: October 4, 2024

The oval at Cebu Technological University Danao Campus is a vital space for both sports and community activities. Serving as a multi-purpose venue, it hosts track and field events, outdoor exercises, and various athletic programs, promoting physical fitness and camaraderie among students.

A standout feature of the oval is its role as a gathering place for major cultural events, such as the Kalikasan Kabataan Kalayaan youth music festival and Karansa sa Danao. The Kalikasan Kabataan Kalayaan festival celebrates youth creativity through music and arts, while also raising awareness about environmental issues. This vibrant event showcases local talent, fostering a sense of community among the youth.

Similarly, Karansa sa Danao highlights the rich cultural heritage of the region, featuring traditional performances, local crafts, and culinary delights. These festivities not only entertain but also promote a deeper appreciation for local traditions and the environment. With its accessibility and versatility, the oval at CTU Danao is more than just a sports facility; it is a dynamic hub for community engagement, cultural celebration, and holistic wellness.
