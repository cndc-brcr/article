The FabLab (Fabrication Laboratory) at Cebu Technological University Danao Campus is a cutting-edge facility that empowers students, faculty, and the local community with essential tools for innovation and prototyping. Equipped with advanced machinery, including 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and electronics workstations, the FabLab provides a space for hands-on learning, creative experimentation, and entrepreneurial projects. As a hub for innovation, the FabLab enables students to transform their ideas into tangible products while developing crucial technical skills. Through workshops and collaborative projects, participants enhance their problem-solving abilities and gain practical experience.

Additionally, the FabLab supports local industries by offering prototype development services and mentorship programs, fostering a culture of collaboration and technological advancement. Emphasizing sustainability, the lab encourages projects focused on environmentally friendly practices, contributing positively to both the community and the environment while nurturing the next generation of innovators.
